How to Play Minesweeper

How to Play

The goal of Minesweeper is to clear a rectangular board without detonating hidden mines.

Steps:

  1. Left-click to reveal a square.
  2. Right-click to place a flag where you suspect a mine is located.
  3. Numbers on revealed squares indicate how many mines are adjacent.
  4. Clear all safe squares to win!
